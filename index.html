Career prediction System using ML

Python-based career prediction system using classification techniques and neural networks.

The various factors that contribute to successful careers in various disciplines are examined in this project after a research. Both qualitative and quantitative data collection and analysis techniques are used in this undertaking. The qualitative research methodology improved access to comprehensive data collection on numerous aspects of job success. . Part I of the study established five relationships between the independent and dependent variables. This study focused on five factors: vocational preparation/efficacy, family impact, personal choices, financial considerations, and instructor influence. Part II of the project aimed to predict an apt career choice considering the personal, behavioural and aptitude data of participants.

CLUSTERING FOR MARKET SEGMENTATION OF BANK CUSTOMERS

Hierarchical clustering is used in the market segmentation of Tesco bank Customers into homogenious groups to identify trends and patterns in the data.. In hierarchical clustering, items group together into a cluster and do not move between the clusters. A sequence of divisions occur, ranging from one cluster holding every object to n clusters with one object each. To obtain the optimum answer, several hierarchical clustering techniques were employed.Categorical variables Housing, Job and marital status, are quantified to be used in clustering models.
